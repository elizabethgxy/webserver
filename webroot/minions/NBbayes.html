Game Tutorial

For this batch of pictures, we ask you for each picture to provide us with:
♦ Your answer regarding whether there is a one-eyed minion.
♦ Your prediction about the percentage of workers who have the same answer as you do.
Your total reward for labeling a picture is a sum of two rewards.

Reward 1: To estimate the quality of your answer, we randomly choose another peer worker who labeled the same picture and reward you with:
♦ 0 point if you and your peer have different answers.
♦ 50/q points if you and your peer have the same answers, where q is the prediction of your peer.
For example, suppose that your peer answers 'Yes, there is a one-eyed minion', and predicts that 80% of workers have the same answer as she does, then you obtain 50/80 = 0.625 for reporting 'Yes, there is a one-eyed minion'.

Reward 2: To estimate the quality of your prediction p, we randomly choose another peer worker who labeled the same picture and reward you with:
♦ 1 - (1-p/100)2 points if you and your peer have same answer.
♦ 1 - (p/100)2 points if you and your peer have different answers.
For example, suppose your answer is 'Yes, there is a one-eyed minion' and you predict that 80% of workers have the same answer as you do. Then, if your peer answers,'Yes, there is a one-eyed minion', you obtain 1-(1-0.8)2 = 0.96. Otherwise, if you peer answers 'No, there is no one-eyed minions', you obtain 1-(1-0.2)2= 0.36.
