Dispicable Me

You are given three batches of pictures, each of them containing 15 pictures of minions. Your task is to find out for each picture whether the picture contains a one-eyed minion.

To make your task more difficult, we cover the pictures with a piece of gray cloth, which you can remove using your mouse (click and move), as shown on the right. Once substantial amount of the cloth is removed from a picture, you can easily see if a one-eyed minion is present in the picture.

We validate your performance using point systems, and each batch of pictures has a different point system. Important: Your bonus payment depends on the total number of points you earn, so it is crucial that you read carefully the descriptions of the point systems, which are provided at the beginning of each batch.
